About Me

I’m an associate professor of Data Science at Coe College in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. Before the move to Iowa, I did my PhD in Applied Mathematics at the University of California in Merced, and a BA and MS, also in Applied Math, at Humboldt State University in Arcata, California.

Research Interests#

While continually expanding, my current interests include:

  • Uncertainty Quantification
  • Data Science
  • Machine Learning (e.g., Deep Learning)
  • High Performance Computing
  • Mathematical Modeling
  • Mathematical Biology
